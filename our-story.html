Sbocciano Flower是一家富有創意的花藝設計工作室。成立於2020年，總部位於台灣。 我們提供高影響力的花束和婚禮佈置，舉辦創意花卉工作坊，並為活動和婚禮提供全面的花卉設計。

Sbocciano Flower is a creative floral design studio. Founded in 2020 and based in Taiwan. We deliver high impact bouquets and wedding arrangements, host creative floral workshops and deliver full scale floral design for events and weddings.

Whether you're getting flowers for home, setting mood for an event, or buying gift for a loved one, we've got you covered!

我們的熱情是通過紋理和意想不到的色彩組合來增強花朵的自然美感。我們選擇不總是保留每種顏色的每種花。取而代之的是，我們會精心選擇一系列搭配效果最佳的鮮花，每週兩次，每次都新鮮交貨。

Our passion is to enhance natural beauty of flowers - through texture and unexpected color combinations. We choose not to have every flower in every color in stock all the time. Instead, we carefully choose a range of flowers that work best together, with fresh deliveries twice a week.

Whether you're getting flowers for home, setting mood for an event, or buying gift for a loved one, we've got you covered!
